The Oracle Criteria
01 / METHODOLOGY
Economic Moat
Durable competitive advantage — brand, switching costs, network effects, or cost advantage
Return on Equity > 15% sustained
Owner Earnings
Free cash flow generative, not just profitable on paper. Cash is fact, profit is opinion.
FCF Yield > 4% · P/FCF < 25
Balance Sheet Fortress
Conservative debt levels. Companies that survive recessions compound through them.
Debt/Equity < 1.0 · Current Ratio > 1.5
Margin of Safety
Graham's core principle: buy below intrinsic value so errors don't become disasters
DCF Discount > 20% · P/E below sector avg
Predictable Earnings
Buffett only buys what he can model 10 years out. Boring beats exciting.
EPS Growth Consistent 5+ years
Capital Allocation
Management that thinks like owners. Buybacks when cheap, dividends when appropriate.
ROIC > WACC · Payout Ratio sustainable
